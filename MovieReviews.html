RRR

RRR is a Telugu action-drama set in the 1920s, inspired by the fictional friendship between two real-life freedom fighters: Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem

Story Line 
RRR follows two freedom fighters, Bheem and Ramaraju, who unknowingly become friends while fighting for different missions. When their truths collide, they clash but ultimately unite to rescue a girl and rise against British rule.
Director SS Rajamouli
Language Telugu
Rating ***
